Artist: Post Malone
Album: Stoney, Stoney (Deluxe)

Track Info: “No Option” is Post Malone’s song to his haters and his fans about the lateness of his album.

In a heartfelt message on Instagram, he told his fans that his album was going to be postponed due to unforeseen complications. He personally apologized on behalf of himself and his team and expressed his displeasure at the setback.

Now that the album is out he now has time to flex on anyone who counted him out when Stoney got delayed, this is his time to shine and no ones getting in his way.
