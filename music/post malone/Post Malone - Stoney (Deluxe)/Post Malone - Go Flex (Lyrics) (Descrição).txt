Artist: Post Malone
Album: Go Flex (Single), Stoney, Stoney (Deluxe)

Track Info: “Go Flex” is a retrospective look at Post Malone’s rise to fame. The song blends several genres into one, featuring Malone’s raps, along with an acoustic guitar.

Malone mentions drama with his girlfriend and alludes to Stone Cold Steve Austin.
